Borrow.me

Make money with your object underused!

The concept is similar to AirBnB where you rent your most precious item, your own appartment.
Borrow.me is a platform to rent your items when you do not use them. Thanks to ressource sharing you can afford to buy brand new items more often and maximize their usage rate.
Or you can borrow new objects without paying the full price. With borrow.me you can afford to use high quality product when you need them.

FAQ

What happen if my item is damaged by the renter?
Firstly, the platform will work like any other exchange platform where you can note the renter and the item rented. But to create a real confidence atmospher, Borrow.me will propose you to take insurances to protect your item.
I want to rent an object that is not in the platform yet, what can I do?
Just create a whish and you will find an owner near to your place. A lot of people does not event know they can make money with their object. Your wish will certainly create the offer!
