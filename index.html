Build your own vocabulary database while reading news, books, blogs, tweets... The words will be automatically translated and added to your list of words. This list is stored on the cloud and will follow you all your life. You can find plenty of vocabulary list and quizlet on the web but this is not fun to learn a language. Start directly to read articles, news and translate the words you don't know. You will automaticaly build the vocabulary list you need. Flashcards and games will be proposed after analysing the frequency of occurence of the word and with the latest result of your tests. Even if you restart years late to learn this language, your vocabulary list will be kept to be more effective. When your level of confidence is high enough start using your vocabulary by chatting with other members You can even meet them if they are located near to you.

Statistics will be computed to measure your language proficieny and learnig velocity Quizz Card and games based on sophisticated algorithm will help you to learn effectively Article, books and films will be advised to continue your language discovery Chat and meet other language learner And everything for free
